About Tactical Adversary.

I believe we're all eternal noobs; we get to learn new things from our day-to-day experience and the beloved contributors of the security community. The goal of Tactical Adversary project is to gain knowledge about different red teaming and adversarial attack simulation tactics, and publish the same under an unique name. I hope this would act as a public journal for me and like-minded folks out-there.
